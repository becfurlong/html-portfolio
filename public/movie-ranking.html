Best Movies according to Bec

My top 3 movies

What's my number

A movie about a girl who reads an article that says one a person has 20 partners the liklihood of them getting a lifelong partner significantly reduces. She then embarks on a mission to find all her previous partners to discover if one of them is has changed enough to be a long term partner
Her journey is one of self discovery where she discovers that she has spent her whole life becoming what other people want rather than embracing what she wants

The Holiday

A sweet movie about two girls who switch houses during the holidays and discover themselves and both find love in the process

Sweet Home Alabama

Another sweet romantic comedy
